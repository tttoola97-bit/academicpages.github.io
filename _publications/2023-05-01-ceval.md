C-Eval is a comprehensive evaluation benchmark designed to assess the capabilities of foundation models on Chinese language tasks. It covers multiple disciplines and difficulty levels, providing a thorough evaluation of model performance in Chinese language understanding and reasoning.

**Authors**: Yuzhen Huang, Yuzhuo Bai, Zhihao Zhu, Junlei Zhang, Jinghan Zhang, Tangjun Su, Junteng Liu, Chuancheng Lv, Yikai Zhang, Jiayi Lei, Yao Fu, Maosong Sun, Junxian He
