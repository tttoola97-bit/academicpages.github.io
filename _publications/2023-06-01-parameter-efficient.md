This research investigates novel methods for composing parameter-efficient modules through arithmetic operations, enabling more flexible and efficient strategies for model adaptation and fine-tuning. The approach allows for dynamic combination of specialized modules to address diverse tasks.

**Authors**: Jinghan Zhang, Shiqi Chen, Junteng Liu, Junxian He
