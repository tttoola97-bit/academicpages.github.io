This paper introduces SynLogic, a novel framework for generating large-scale verifiable reasoning data to improve logical reasoning capabilities in language models. The approach focuses on creating synthetic data that can be automatically verified, enabling scalable training of reasoning abilities.

**Authors**: Junteng Liu, Yuanxiang Fan, Zhuo Jiang, Han Ding, Yongyi Hu, Chi Zhang, Yiqi Shi, Shitong Weng, Aili Chen, Shiqi Chen, Yunan Huang, Mozhi Zhang, Pengyu Zhao, Junjie Yan, Junxian He
